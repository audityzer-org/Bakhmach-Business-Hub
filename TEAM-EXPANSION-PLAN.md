## Hiring Strategy

### Sourcing Channels
1. **Community First**: Promote top contributors from community
   - Early hiring advantage: 30% cost reduction
   - Proven commitment to project values
   - Target: 50% of hires from community

2. **Networks**: Founder/team personal networks
   - Faster hiring process
   - Better cultural fit assessment
   - Target: 25% of hires

3. **Job Boards**: AngelList, YCombinator Jobs, GitHub Jobs
   - Reach tech-savvy audience
   - Attract mission-driven talent
   - Target: 15% of hires

4. **Agencies/Recruiters**: For specialist roles (CFO, VP Marketing)
   - Professional search capability
   - Industry connections
   - Target: 10% of hires

## Culture & Values

### Core Hiring Criteria
1. **Mission Alignment**: Belief in cooperative model & well-being
2. **Ownership Mindset**: Take initiative, solve problems
3. **Collaborative Spirit**: Work well with diverse team
4. **Learning Agility**: Adapt quickly in startup environment
5. **Communication**: Clear, transparent, honest

### Onboarding Process
1. **Week 1**: Culture, values, systems orientation
2. **Week 2-3**: Technical setup, project assignment
3. **Month 1**: 30-60-90 day plan with manager
4. **Month 2**: First performance check-in
5. **Month 3**: 3-month evaluation & feedback

### Team Development
- Monthly 1:1s with manager
- Quarterly OKR reviews
- Annual performance reviews
- Learning & development budget: $2K-5K per person/year
- Internal knowledge sharing sessions
